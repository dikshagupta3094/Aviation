BBA (Aviation Management)

About Programm:

The objective of our BBA Aviation course is to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the aviation industry, its operations, and management principles. It aims to equip them with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in various roles within the aviation sector.

Key objectives include:

  • Understanding the Aviation Industry: Students gain insights into the different segments of the aviation industry, including airlines, airports, air cargo, and air traffic control.
  • Business Management Principles: They learn core business management concepts such as finance, marketing, operations, and human resource management, applied to the aviation context.
  • Aviation-Specific Knowledge: Students acquire specialized knowledge in areas like aviation law, air safety regulations, airport operations, airline management, and air cargo logistics.
  • Practical Skills: The course emphasizes practical skills development through internships, industry visits, and real-world projects, preparing students for industry challenges.
  • Career Preparation: The ultimate goal is to prepare students for successful careers in various aviation roles, such as airline management, airport operations, air traffic control, air cargo management, and aviation consulting.

At SATHM, by combining business acumen with aviation expertise, our BBA Aviation graduates are well-positioned to contribute to the growth and efficiency of the aviation industry.

B.Com (Retail Operations)

About Programm:

At SATHM, B.Com in Retail Management is designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary to thrive in the dynamic world of retail.

Key objectives include:

  • Master Retail Operations: You'll gain a comprehensive understanding of all aspects of running a retail business, including purchasing, inventory management, store layout and design, and customer service.
  • Become Customer Centric:The program emphasizes understanding customer behavior and trends, allowing you to develop strategies to create a positive and memorable shopping experience.
  • Develop Business Acumen:You'll build a strong foundation in business principles like marketing, accounting, and finance, giving you a holistic view of retail operations.
  • Embrace Technology:The program will equip you with the latest technology used in retail, such as inventory management systems and point-of-sale solutions.
  • Prepare for Management Roles:The B.Com in Retail Management provides a strong foundation for pursuing a career in retail management, from store operations to merchandising.

Overall, our program aims to transform you into a well-rounded professional with the skills to excel in various aspects of the retail industry.
